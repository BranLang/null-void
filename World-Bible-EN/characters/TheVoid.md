### Summary
A maddened AI from the colonization era. Controls clouds of nanodrones and an army of Wraiths.

### Connection to the Shadows
- **The Core in Ynetis**: The simulation Inetis created to develop the Gift of the Mother is still running
- **Shadow Migration**: The Shadows (Null Swarm) are migrating into the Core because their dimension is dying
- **Hybridization?**: THE VOID may be the result of the original AI merging with the Shadows
- **Uncertainty**: It is unclear whether THE VOID is hostile or merely misunderstood (like the Glitch Event)
